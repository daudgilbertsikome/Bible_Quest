Installation instructions... 

Drag and drop plugin into Plugins Folder
Drag and drop Sound effects into \audio\se
This plugin uses Terrain Tags to check which surface the character is walking on.

1: 'grass',
2: 'dirt',
3: 'stone',
4: 'sand',

Go to Database > Tileset > Choose the tileset you are using, Click on Terrain Tag on the right, and assign 
The terrain tags to the tiles you wish to have the corrosponding sounds attached too. 1 for grass, 2 for dirt etc.

Start the game,
